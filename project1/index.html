ABOUT ME

EDUARDO HUERTA-MERCADO

Hi! I am Eduardo Huerta-Mercado. Let me tell you a bit about myself. I was born and raised in Lima, Peru. Currently, I am a fourth-year EECS student at the University of California, Berkeley, with a minor in Data Science. I've founded an NGO called UTK United Technologies for Kids and a Berkeley student organization, INvent, which promotes STEM education in underprivileged communities and developing countries. So far, UTK has inspired more than 10,000 kids in eleven countries. This summer, I interned at Salesforce - Tableau as a software engineer, where I worked in the Metrics team to develop a new feature that compares a KPI with historical data for the mobile and desktop versions. As a result, Tableau users can now author metrics with historical comparisons on mobile and server.
